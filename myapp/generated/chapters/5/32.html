Tax Incidence - Summary Data

When Demand Is: Perfectly Inelastic - Buyers pay all of the tax. Inelastic - Buyers pay more of the tax. Elastic - Sellers pay more of the tax. Perfectly Elastic - Sellers pay all of the tax. When Supply Is: Perfectly Inelastic - Sellers pay all of the tax. Inelastic - Sellers pay more of the tax. Elastic - Buyers pay more of the tax. Perfectly Elastic - Buyers pay all of the tax.

Chapter 2, 1. Willingness to Buy Products or Services

Depends on:

  • Ability to pay
  • Comparative benefits and costs
  • Availability of substitutes

Minecraft GTNH Application:

  • Players must decide how to spend their resources (iron, rubber, redstone) on tech progression.
  • Example: Is it worth buying/making an LV Macerator instead of using basic Steam Crushers?

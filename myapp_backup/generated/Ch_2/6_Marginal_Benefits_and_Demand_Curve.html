Chapter 2, 6. Marginal Benefits and Demand Curve

  • Marginal benefits change with circumstances.
  • Willingness to pay depends on marginal benefits.
  • Abundance → Marginal benefit low.
  • Scarcity → Marginal benefit high.

Minecraft GTNH Application:

  • If coal is abundant, investing in better furnaces is less urgent.
  • If coal is scarce, an LV Electric Furnace becomes more valuable.

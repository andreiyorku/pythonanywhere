Chapter 2, 8. Increase vs. Decrease in Demand

  • Increase in Demand → Rightward shift of demand curve.
  • Decrease in Demand → Leftward shift of demand curve.

Minecraft GTNH Application:

  • If LV circuits become cheaper, players will transition faster into LV tech.
  • If Steam Boilers get nerfed (less efficient), demand for LV generators increases.

Chapter 2, 10. Law of Demand and Externalities

  • Law of Demand: If price rises, quantity demanded decreases.
  • Implicit Costs: Hidden opportunity costs.
  • Externalities: Unintended effects of private choices on others.

Minecraft GTNH Application:

  • Negative externality: Coal pollution makes biofuel a more attractive alternative.
  • Positive externality: Investing in better power tech improves base efficiency long-term.

Chapter 2, 9. Factors Affecting Demand

  • Increase in demand occurs when:
    • Preference for a product increases.
    • Substitutes become more expensive.
    • Complements become cheaper.
    • Income increases (for normal goods).
    • Expected future price rises.
    • More consumers (server economy in multiplayer).

Minecraft GTNH Application:

  • If Steam burn rate is increased, demand for LV generators rises.
  • If copper becomes rare, players might delay LV transition and stick to Steam machines.

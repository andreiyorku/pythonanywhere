Chapter 3, 6. Supply Curve and Marginal Cost

- Supply curve shows the relationship between price and quantity supplied, assuming other factors remain constant.

- Marginal cost curve shows the minimum price a business will accept to cover all marginal opportunity costs of production.

Minecraft GTNH Application: The cost of refining ores (steam vs. LV-tier machines) changes depending on resource efficiency.
